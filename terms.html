Terms & Conditions

By enrolling your child, you agree to the following guidelines to ensure a safe and joyful experience for everyone.

1. Supervision & Safety

  • Outdoor workshops: Parents/carers must supervise their children at all times and are fully responsible for their safety.
  • Indoor workshops: Children are supervised by Bright Bloom staff.
  • Children must follow facilitator instructions.
  • Bright Bloom is not responsible for injury, loss, or damage outside staff supervision.

2. Photos & Videos

Photos or videos of your child or their artwork may be used for promotion, social media, or our website. If you do not want your child’s image used, please email us before the session.

3. Refunds & Transfers

No refunds are provided, but tickets may be transferred to another person via email.

Reschedule fees:
  • 7+ days before – free
  • 2–7 days before – 25%
  • 1 day before – 50%
  • No-shows forfeit booking

4. Workshop Guidelines

  • Children should participate independently in indoor workshops.
  • Nut-free zone: Parents are responsible for any outside food.
  • Children must wear enclosed shoes and clothes suitable for messy play.
  • Please arrive on time (but not early).

5. Child Safety

Indoor facilitators hold Working with Children Checks, Police Clearance, and first aid training. Children are supervised at all times during indoor sessions.

6. Enrolment

Your child’s spot is confirmed after full payment. Bright Bloom may refuse, suspend, or end enrolment if necessary.
