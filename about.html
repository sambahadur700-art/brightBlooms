Our Petals Learning

Bright Bloom is a thoughtfully curated early learning space where creativity and imagination flourish.

Welcome to Bright Bloom

Bright Bloom offers weekly creative learning sessions designed to inspire young minds through art, craft, and play. Our programs invite children aged 2.5 to 6 years to explore, create, and express themselves in a calm and nurturing environment.

Led by a qualified early childhood teacher, our research-informed program supports early development and school readiness through play-based learning. Sessions are offered in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Hello and Welcome!

Meet Jay

Hi, I’m Jay, a passionate Early Childhood Teacher with over 9 years of experience working with children in Melbourne.

I’ve always dreamed of creating something truly for children—a space where they can explore, create, and learn in a way that feels natural and joyful. I believe every child is unique, capable, and curious.

"I can’t wait to meet you and your little one!"

Our Philosophy

Inspired by the Reggio Emilia approach, our programs encourage exploration, self-expression, and discovery—both indoors and in nature. We see every child as a unique learner, and provide hands-on experiences that nurture confidence and imagination.
